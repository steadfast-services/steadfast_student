What You're Actually Facing
The Honest Challenges of Enrollment From Your Region
🏛️
Heightened Consular Scrutiny
Consular officers at U.S. embassies in moderate-risk countries apply stricter questioning standards. Section 214(b) presumption of immigrant intent is actively enforced — applicants must prove strong home-country ties.
Major cause of denials
📁
Documentation Complexity
Schools require precise financial verification documents. Errors in bank statement formatting, affidavit of support wording, or sponsor letter structure can stall or kill an I-20 before the visa stage is even reached.
Avoidable with guidance
🎓
University Matching Errors
Applying to schools that are a poor academic fit — either too selective or without strong international pipelines — wastes time and signals poor planning to consulates reviewing the application record.
Undermines credibility
🗣️
Visa Interview Unpreparedness
Most denied applicants are rejected in under 3 minutes. Without practiced, concise answers to predictable consular questions, even qualified students stumble on language, confidence, or clarity.
Most overlooked risk
