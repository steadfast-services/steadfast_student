# Email Template: Alumni Ambassador Program Invitation

**Audience:** Successfully placed students, 3-6 months after their arrival in the U.S.
**Sender:** `advisors@steadfaststudentservices.com`
**Objective:** Convert successful alumni into referral partners, leveraging their trust and success story to acquire new, high-quality leads.

---

### Subject Line Options:
*   **Option A (Personal & Intriguing):** Checking In from Steadfast + A Special Invitation
*   **Option B (Benefit-focused):** A note from Steadfast & a thank you bonus
*   **Option C (Direct):** An Invitation to the Steadfast Alumni Ambassador Program

---

### Email Body

Hi [Student First Name],

Hope you are settling in well at [University Name] and that your first semester is off to a great start!

Everyone here at Steadfast was so proud to be a part of your journey to the U.S. We know the process wasn't easy, but your hard work and preparation paid off. Your success story is a powerful example for other students from [Student's Home Country] who are just beginning to navigate the same challenges you faced.

**Why I'm Reaching Out:**
Because you've successfully been through the entire process, you're in a unique position to offer credible, trusted advice. That’s why I’m personally inviting you to join our exclusive **Steadfast Alumni Ambassador Program**.

**You Help, You Earn. It's That Simple:**

1.  **You Share:** You share your positive experience with friends, family, or community members who are looking to study in the U.S.
2.  **They Mention You:** When a new student signs up for our Premium or Elite package and mentions your name during their consultation, we tag them as your referral.
3.  **You Earn:** Once your referred student successfully pays their tuition deposit and enrolls, we send you a **$100 referral bonus** via PayPal or Wise as a thank you.

There are no obligations and no time commitments. It's simply a way for you to help others from your community follow in your footsteps, and for us to thank you for trusting us with your own journey.

If you’re interested, just reply "I'm in" to this email, and we'll officially add you to our Ambassador list. That's all it takes.

We're incredibly proud of what you've accomplished and look forward to hopefully welcoming you to the ambassador team.

All the best,

The Team at Steadfast Student Services
