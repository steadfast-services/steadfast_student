The Low-Risk Funnel (Backend Commission Model): For countries like Colombia, Brazil, South Korea, and Taiwan, adopt an onboarding-heavy model. Because approval chances are high, offer free or low-cost initial consultations. Rely on the master aggregator commission check ($500 to $2,000 per student) upon their successful arrival at the university campus.The High-Risk Funnel (Upfront Retainer Model): For countries like Nepal, Bangladesh, Nigeria, and Pakistan, do not rely on backend commissions. The 70%+ rejection risk means you could work for months and receive zero commission. Charge these students a non-refundable upfront "Admissions Consulting & Portfolio Build Fee" ($300 - $700). If they pass the visa interview, your aggregator commission is a pure bonus.3. Service Framework (The End-to-End Lifecycle)You will handle the student from initial contact until they step onto their U.S. campus:Phase 1: Pre-Screening & Evaluation: Audit the applicant's GPA transcripts, English test profiles (IELTS, TOEFL, or Duolingo), and liquid financial capacity.Phase 2: University Alignment: Match the student with partner public research systems (e.g., ASU, University of Delaware, University of Arizona) via your master aggregator portal.Phase 3: Document Assembly: Edit personal statements, format professional resumes, collect letters of recommendation, and oversee the formal application portal submission.Phase 4: I-20 & SEVIS Processing: Guide the family through the financial verification required by the school's international student office to issue the Form I-20.Phase 5: The Visa Defense Program: Conduct three distinct rounds of mock consular interviews. Target specific objections under Section 214(b) by practicing answers about their home country ties and exact career paths.4. Revenue & Monetization StrategyDirect-to-Consumer Client Pricing:Low-Risk Package: Free application management with a mandatory $250 visa prep deposit (refundable only if the visa is denied).High-Risk Package: $500 flat fee paid upfront for full application submission, transcript translation audits, and complete visa defense training.B2B Commission Structure: Secure sub-agent credentials through global platforms like ApplyBoard, Adventus, or Shorelight. Expect average commission yields of 10% to 15% of the student's first-year tuition payout, split between the master network and your agency.5. Compliance & Security Protocol (U.S. Based)1099 Financial Management: All incoming funds must pass through clear U.S. tax paths. Issue a Form W-9 to your aggregator partner and file taxes using Schedule C (Form 1040).Work Authorization Check: If operating on F-1 OPT, verify that your business description matches your major CIP code. Ensure your school's DSO logs at least 20 hours per week of active business development inside your SEVP portal.Ethical Boundaries: Never write essays directly for students, forge bank documentation, or guarantee visa issuance. Maintain clear, signed client agreements stating that visa decisions rest entirely with the U.S. Department of State.
