The Honest Challenges of Enrollment From Your Region

Heightened Consular Scrutiny
Consular officers at U.S. embassies in moderate-risk countries apply stricter questioning standards. Section 214(b) presumption of immigrant intent is actively enforced.
Major cause of denials
📁
Documentation Complexity
Schools require precise financial verification documents. Errors in bank statement formatting or sponsor letter structure can stall or kill an I-20 before the visa stage.
Avoidable with guidance
🎓
University Matching Errors
Applying to schools that are a poor academic fit wastes time and signals poor planning to consulates reviewing the application record.
Undermines credibility
🗣
Visa Interview Unpreparedness
Most denied applicants are rejected in under 3 minutes. Without practiced, concise answers to predictable consular questions, even qualified students stumble.
Most overlooked risk
