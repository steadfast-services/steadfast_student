The Steadfast 5-Phase Enrollment Journey
1
Pre-Screening & Eligibility Evaluation
We audit your GPA, English test scores (IELTS / TOEFL / Duolingo), financial capacity, and academic goals. Every applicant receives a Placement Viability Score before we commit to working together. Honest assessment upfront saves everyone time.
2
University Alignment & Match Strategy
We build a balanced portfolio of 8–10 schools — reach, target, and safety — all with active F-1 pipelines. Partners include ASU, University of Delaware, University of Arizona, University of Utah, and more. We submit directly through aggregator portals for fast processing.
3
Document Assembly & Application Submission
We coach you through your Statement of Purpose, format your resume to U.S. academic standards, coordinate Letters of Recommendation timelines, and oversee every portal submission. We guide — you write. Full academic integrity, always.
4
I-20 & SEVIS Processing
Once admitted, we guide your family through every financial verification document the school's International Student Office requires to issue your Form I-20. We also ensure your SEVIS record is created and the I-901 fee is paid before your DS-160 visa application is filed.
5
Pre-Departure Orientation
A 90-minute virtual session: airport arrival, campus check-in, DSO reporting, Social Security Number, banking setup, health insurance, and cultural transition coaching. You'll land in the U.S. knowing exactly what to do on Day 1.
